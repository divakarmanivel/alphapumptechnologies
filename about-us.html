Who we are

Established in 1987, we began in fabrication works, motors, and pump spares. In 2004, we evolved into a pump manufacturing unit operating as Alpha Pump Technologies under the FINE ALPHA brand.

Our well-qualified, technically proficient, and highly motivated team of engineers has enabled us to achieve consistent growth and success. We believe that quality assurance means getting it right the first time.

Application areas

With transparent business policies and a positive approach towards offering excellent quality motors and pumps, we have acquired the trust of valued clients across agricultural, domestic, industrial, and commercial sectors. Our professionals conduct regular market surveys to deliver products that match real-world needs.
